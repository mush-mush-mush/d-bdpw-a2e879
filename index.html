Jakarta’s Old Batavia

Tucked away amidst the hustle and bustle of Indonesia’s modern capital is Kota Tua Jakarta, the Old Batavia area of the city. The building that houses the museum is in itself a historical landmark since it was used as the Batavia City Hall by the Dutch colonists. Take a journey with Little Steps through this interesting area in Jakarta packed with unique museums, gardens, and more.
